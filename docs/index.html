04How a day is made

Flow + warp. Particles advect through a domain-warped value-noise field, drawn as glowing ribbons with log-density tone mapping — the source of the "designed chaos."

Palette. Either a cosine palette (the formula in §02) or colours sampled from a reference image, assigned with spatial clumping.

Composition. The date seed picks marble (edge-to-edge) or focal (a radial explosion with negative space), then a depth-layered overlay adds rings, dots and diamonds.

Selection. The engine renders many candidates and keeps the best, judged by a free heuristic or an optional learned aesthetic model. The winner becomes the day.
